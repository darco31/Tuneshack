# The TuneShack

### About
* The Tuneshack is a blog website developed to encourage lovers of all types and genres of music to sign up and talk freely about their love of music, their favorite bands or the best concert they have ever been to. The blog can used to post dates and venues for upcoming concerts. Every bod is welcome and encouraged to post and interact with each others posts weather leaving comments or just liking a post.
